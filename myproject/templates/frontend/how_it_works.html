Recommended Usage Flow

Step By Step
1. Open the portal and log in using one of the test users.
2. As `admin_test`, review dashboard, users, hospitals, alerts, and audit logs.
3. As `nurse_test` or `doctor_test`, go to OHC Visit Form and register a visit for an employee.
4. Open Diagnosis Entry, add diagnosis details, prescriptions, fitness decision, and follow-up date.
5. If the case is serious or critical, the system creates an AHC referral automatically.
6. Use Referral Page and Hospital Selection to assign the employee to an external hospital.
7. Upload medical reports in Reports and create payment records in Payments.
8. Review Employee Health History, Disease Trends, and Department Stats in Reports & Analytics.
9. As `ehs_test`, `hr_test`, or `kam_test`, review alerts, audit logs, and compliance insight pages.

Who Uses What

Roles
Admin: full configuration, dashboard, finance, audit, and control.
Nurse: OHC intake, injury/illness records, basic follow-up coordination, and inventory-linked workflow.
On Site Doctor: diagnosis, prescriptions, referrals, tests, and clinical follow-up decisions.
EHS: compliance dashboard, inspections, alerts, audit trail, and downloadable statutory reporting.
HR: employee fitness review, restrictions, certificate follow-up, and workforce health stats.
KAM: client oversight, review dashboards, compliance visibility, and management MIS.
Employee: own health history, referral progress, reports, and invoice/payment view.
